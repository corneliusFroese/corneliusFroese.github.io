Previous Projects

Press Play Hackforge

This site was made in one week. I participated in an event called Press Play, ran by HackForge. Using open data from the City of Windsor, Clara Benedek and I, set out to show the flaws with the Windsor Transit System.

Before the mid week meeting, I had already come up with a design for the website as well as started to code some basic layout. At the meeting mid week, we felt pretty confident about what was coming together for the website and went over some content that was to shown and what exactly we were trying to show.

When the end of the week presentation came by, we we're satisfied with what we had completed in the time frame and came away from this name Best Overall Team.
